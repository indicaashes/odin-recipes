Creole Okra

What is Creole Okra?

This dish is a Louisana comfort food. It is a simple okra soup with a louisiana twist to it.

What Do You Need to Make Creole Okra?

  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 2 pounds okra , sliced
  • 1 onion , diced finely
  • 1 orange pepper , diced
  • 1 can (28oz) diced tomatoes
  • 2 tsp cajun seasoning

How to Make Creole Okra?

  1. Heat oil in a large sized dutch oven or saucepan.
  2. Add the sliced okra, diced onion and pepper. Saut√© over medium heat stirring often, until the onions and translucent. About 7-8 minutes.
  3. Stir in the diced tomatoes and seasoning. Bring to a boil and lower heat to a simmer.
  4. Cover with lid and let the okra and vegetables simmer for 30-45 minutes. Stir occasionally to ensure that it does not stick to the bottom of the dutch oven.
